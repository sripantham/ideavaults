FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Who can submit ideas to the platform?

Anyone with an innovative idea can submit to our platform. We welcome students, professionals, entrepreneurs, and creative thinkers from all backgrounds.

Is there a cost to use the platform?

Basic idea submission and feedback are free. Premium features like advanced mentorship and certain competitions may have entry fees, but many opportunities are completely free.

How do I protect my intellectual property?

We have robust IP protection policies. You can choose what level of detail to share publicly, and we offer NDA options for sensitive discussions with mentors and investors.

What kind of funding opportunities are available?

We connect innovators with various funding sources including grants, angel investors, venture capital, and corporate sponsorships. The type of funding depends on your idea's stage and industry.

How does the student project support work?

Students receive specialized mentorship, access to academic resources, and connections to industry partners. We also offer specific competitions and funding opportunities designed for academic projects.
